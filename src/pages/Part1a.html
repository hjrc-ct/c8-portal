    Limited Google Cloud Console Access

    As a c8-labs participant, you are granted only the permissions required for your hands-on lab activities. Some pages in the Google Cloud Console may therefore display permission-related messages.

    For example, if you navigate to Kubernetes Engine → Clusters, you may see:
    You need additional access to the project: c8-labs

    This is expected behavior and is part of the security model for the lab environment. Your access is intentionally restricted to your assigned namespace and the resources needed to complete the exercises.

    If you encounter this message while following the lab guide or are unable to complete a required exercise, please contact the lab administrator.
