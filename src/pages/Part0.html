Welcome to Bengaluru community chapter event!

We are pleased to offer a hands-on, self-paced learning experience designed for practitioners working with modern cloud-native architectures.

This program is delivered on Google Cloud Platform (GKE), providing a real-world environment to explore Kubernetes-based deployments. Refer to the Know Your Environment section for detailed setup and access information.

Participants will provision and deploy a fully functional Camunda application from the ground up, gaining practical insights into deployment patterns, infrastructure configuration, and runtime considerations.
