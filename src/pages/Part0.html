Welcome to the Community Chapter Bengaluru hands‑on lab !

This self‑paced program is designed for practitioners working with modern cloud‑native architectures who want practical experience running Camunda on Kubernetes.

The lab environment runs on Google Kubernetes Engine (GKE) in Google Cloud Platform, providing a realistic setup to explore Kubernetes-based deployments. Refer to the Know Your Environment section for detailed setup, access instructions, and cluster information.

Participants will provision and deploy a fully functional Camunda application from the ground up, gaining practical insights into deployment patterns, infrastructure configuration, component dependencies and runtime considerations.
